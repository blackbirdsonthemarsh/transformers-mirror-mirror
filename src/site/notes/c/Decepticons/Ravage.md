Ravage has always been a chatterbox, from the moment he was built, and he’s never particularly cared whether anyone is actually listening. Ravage has always been an upbeat ‘bot, managing to be even more optimistic than his [[Cybertron/Cybertronian Biology/Deployers\|deployer]], [[c/Decepticons/High Command/Soundwave\|c/Decepticons/High Command/Soundwave]]— a hard feat to manage! 

One shouldn’t mistake excitability for stupidity, nor should they always believe first impressions. Ravage is incredibly intelligent, driven, and fiercely loyal.
