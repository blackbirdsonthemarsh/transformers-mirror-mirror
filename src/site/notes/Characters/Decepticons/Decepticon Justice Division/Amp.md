An [[Cybertron/Cybertronian Biology/Outlier\|outlier]] with a notoriously dangerous — and notoriously difficult to control — ability, Amp had always struggled to find his place in the world, with his powers costing him jobs, relationships, and almost guaranteeing he could never live a normal life. Where could someone who ran the risk of destroying every piece of technology he touched find work? He had only ever felt tolerated at best, and was often treated like either a nuisance, or a ticking time bomb. This was until he joined the [[Cybertron/History/The Great War/Factions/Decepticons/Decepticon\|Decepticons]], who were the first people he’d ever felt truly welcome around. With them, he learned to not just control, but master his abilities, and they went from something he was ashamed of to a source of pride.

Amp found a [[Characters/Decepticons/Decepticon Justice Division/Damus\|kindred spirit]] in the form of the leader of the [[Decepticon Justice Division\|Decepticon Justice Division]], whose powers had put him in a very similar position. Amp’s own abilities and the skills he’d learned during the war led to Damus inviting him to become a founding member of the [[Decepticon Justice Division\|Decepticon Justice Division]], where he served as its communications officer. 
##### Notes
- He’s a cat person.
- Outliers whose abilities disrupt technology tend to have very few employment options unless they can get their powers under control. Many lived at the edge of poverty, because the vast majority of jobs were inaccessible.
