An elite [[Cybertron/History/The Great War/Factions/Autobots/Autobot\|Autobot]] strike force under the command of [[Characters/Autobots/Elita One\|Elita One]]. 

Its original roster was comprised of:
- [[Chromia\|Chromia]]
- [[Firestar\|Firestar]]
- [[Characters/Moonracer\|Characters/Moonracer]]
- [[Greenlight\|Greenlight]]
- [[Lancer\|Lancer]]

After Lancer’s death, she was replaced with a newcomer, [[Arcee\|Arcee]]. [[Characters/Camiens/Windblade\|Windblade]] worked alongside the Shield, though she was never a formal member. 
##### Notes
---
- This is the Mirror Mirror counterpart of [Elita One’s team](https://tfwiki.net/wiki/Female_Autobots) from [The Search for Alpha Trion](https://tfwiki.net/wiki/The_Search_for_Alpha_Trion). Instead of having been accidentally left behind on Cybertron, keeping up a seemingly hopeless fight with whatever they can scrape together, they are an elite force answering only to Optimus Prime himself. 
- They originally went by a Mirror Mirror-original name, the Furies, but after Legacy finally gave them a name — that being the Shield of Solus — I decided to go with that.
