Its original roster was comprised of:
- [[Chromia\|Chromia]]
- [[Firestar\|Firestar]]
- [[Characters/Moonracer\|Characters/Moonracer]]
- [[Greenlight\|Greenlight]]
- [[Lancer\|Lancer]]

After Lancer’s death, she was replaced with a newcomer, [[Arcee\|Arcee]]. [[Characters/Camiens/Windblade\|Windblade]] worked alongside the Furies, though she was never a formal member.
