A punishment practiced during the [[Cybertron/History/The Primacy/Primacy\|Primacy]], first reserved for traitors, then used for criminals, then for anyone the state [[Galvatron\|deemed]] [[Characters/Decepticons/High Command/Soundwave\|a]] [[Characters/Decepticons/High Command/Megatron\|threat]], or simply wanted to [[Characters/Decepticons/High Command/Shockwave\|punish.]]

The punishment was first conceived during the reign of [[Characters/Primacy/Nominus Prime\|Nominus Prime]], as a means of punishing those who had collaborated with the invading [[Quintessons\|Quintessons]], marking them as beings to be ostracized. However, it was quickly used as a means of ostracizing 

The reason empurata exists is not just to socially ostracize, but to deny victims the ability to fully participate in society. Most Cybertronians speak non-verbal languages, and said languages — ranging from sign language to chirolinguistics — are deeply important to most cultures on Cybertron. In the case of most Cybertronian sign languages, the grammar comes from the face. The cheap claws given to someone after an empurata lack the sensors for chirolinguistics. Empuratas have no means of tasting food, and their only means of refueling is by secondary refueling ports (typically located in the arms or the torso). They can no longer even enjoy the food they eat.

It serves a symbolic purpose, too. Obviously, the removal of the face depersonalizes the ‘bot, but it is a removal of an identity on an even deeper level. The first parts of a Cybertronian to take shape are the face and hands. They are the first parts of a ‘bot that are unique to them, truly theirs. It is a literal removal of the first parts of you that made you, you.

Empurata was also a notably cheap process, only growing cheaper. The original proposal involved removing the head, but that was deemed too expensive. Instead, the face was ripped out of the head, and replaced with a low-quality screen that was hooked up to the existing optical feed. Originally, empuratas had three claws, but late in the reign of [[Characters/Primacy/Sentinel Prime\|Sentinel Prime]], he and the [[Cybertron/History/The Primacy/Senate\|Senate]] decided that it would be far cheaper to give empuratas a pair of pincers instead.
