The seat of the [[Cybertron/History/The Primacy/Primacy\|reigning Prime]] and the [[Cybertron/History/The Primacy/Senate\|Senate]], built during the reign of [[Characters/Primacy/Zeta Prime\|Zeta Prime]], that was later repurposed as the seat of [[Cybertron/History/The Great War/Factions/Autobots/Autobot\|Autobot]] High Command. The Citadel is a shining spire in the heart of [[Cybertron/Polities/Iacon\|Iacon]], surrounded by the finest things in the city, from the Stellar Galleries to the Tower of Pion. At the very top, overlooking the city, are its meeting halls, covered in murals depicting triumphs of ages past. 
 
 ##### Notes
- It’s a combination of the [Citadel](https://tfwiki.net/wiki/Citadel_(building)) and [Grand Imperium](https://tfwiki.net/wiki/Grand_Imperium) from IDw 2005, the [High Council Pavilions](https://tfwiki.net/wiki/High_Council_Pavilions) from Dreamwave, and the [High Council Tower](https://tfwiki.net/wiki/High_Council_Tower) from Aligned. I wanted features from all of those buildings, and rather than trying to keep track of several, I merged them into one.
