A sea comprised of layers upon layers of thick, opaque gases. Beneath the surface lies a wholly unique — and wholly isolated — ecosystem, dependent on the heat welling up from the planet’s core. The surface is a spiderweb of cracks and canyons, some reaching down to Primus himself. Odd shapes jut up from the depths, resembling twisting spires. 

Legend has it that a [[Cybertron/History/Age of the Primes/Titan\|Titan]] dwells in its depths.
