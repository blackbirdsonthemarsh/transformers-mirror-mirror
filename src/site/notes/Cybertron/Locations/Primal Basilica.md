A grand building constructed in [[Cybertron/Polities/Iacon\|Iacon]] after the [[Cybertron/History/The Quintesson War/Quintesson War\|Quintesson War]] to serve as a memorial to all those who had fallen, and a tomb for the [[Characters/Nova Prime\|one]] who had led them to victory. It later housed the bodies of [[Characters/Primacy/Nominus Prime\|Nominus Prime]] and [[Characters/Primacy/Zeta Prime\|Zeta Prime]]. It was at the steps of the Primal Basilica that Nominus Prime announced the unification of Cybertron. 

It was at the feet of the Primal Basilica that [[Characters/Primacy/Sentinel Prime\|Sentinel Prime]] fell, during the last days of the [[Cybertron/History/The Primacy/Decepticon Uprising\|Decepticon Uprising]], marking the end of the [[Cybertron/History/The Primacy/Primacy\|Primacy]].
