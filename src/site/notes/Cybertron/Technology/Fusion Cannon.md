[[Characters/Decepticons/High Command/Megatron\|Megatron’s]] signature weapon and [[Characters/Decepticons/High Command/Starscream\|Starscream]]’s most terrifying invention, an arm-mounted cannon powered by antimatter siphoned from a black hole. The cannon has a range of twelve miles, and one shot is capable of obliterating a small town. 

Starscream later produced a twin-barreled variant for [[Characters/Decepticons/Damus\|Tarn]]. Tarn’s fusion cannons have an improved cooling system, derived from the system found on [[Seeker\|Seeker]] ion cannons, allowing the cannons to maintain a faster rate of fire. While they were designed to only fire one shot at a time, it is possible to fire both barrels at once. 
##### Notes
- While [[Characters/Decepticons/High Command/Starscream\|Starscream]] inventing the cannon is original to Mirror Mirror, him giving it to Megatron as a gift is a nod to IDW, it being powered by black hole sci-fi nonsense comes from the original G1 bio, as do its other specs. 
- [[Characters/Decepticons/Decepticon Intelligence/Prowl\|Prowl]], whose life can only be described as patently insane, considers Starscream a nutcase for having invented such a thing. 
- Megatron’s fusion cannon is largely inspired by its desciption in his [G1 bio](https://tfwiki.net/mediawiki/images2/b/bc/MegsMarvTFU.jpg).  Tarn’s, design-wise, are inspired by an old piece of Transformers: Hero (the show that became Animated) concept art of a tank with a Decepticon mask and two fusion cannons. While that design has nothing to do with Tarn (Alex Milne had not seen it until after More Than Meets the Eye had been published) I have taken quite a bit of inspiration from it, as I prefer it to his IDW design.
