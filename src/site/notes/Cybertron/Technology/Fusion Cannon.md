[[Characters/Decepticons/High Command/Megatron\|Megatron’s]] signature weapon and [[Characters/Decepticons/High Command/Starscream\|Starscream]]’s most terrifying invention, an arm-mounted cannon powered by antimatter siphoned from a black hole. The cannon has a range of twelve miles, and one shot is capable of obliterating a small town. 

Starscream later produced a twin-barreled variant for [[Characters/Decepticons/Damus\|Tarn]].
##### Notes
---
- While [[Characters/Decepticons/High Command/Starscream\|Starscream]] inventing the cannon is original to Mirror Mirror, him giving it to Megatron as a gift is a nod to IDW, it being powered by black hole sci-fi nonsense comes from the original G1 bio, as do its other specs. 
- [[Characters/Decepticons/Decepticon Intelligence/Prowl\|Prowl]], whose life can only be described as patently insane, considers Starscream a nutcase for having invented such a thing. 
- Megatron’s fusion cannon is largely inspired by its desciption in his [G1 bio](https://tfwiki.net/mediawiki/images2/b/bc/MegsMarvTFU.jpg).
