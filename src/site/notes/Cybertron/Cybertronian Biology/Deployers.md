“Deployer” refers to [[Cybertronians\|Cybertronians]] who have chosen to form symbiotic partnerships with smaller members of their species. 

The only notable deployer in Mirror Mirror is [[Characters/Decepticons/High Command/Soundwave\|Soundwave]], partnered with [[Characters/Decepticons/Ravage\|Ravage]], [[Characters/Decepticons/Decepticon Intelligence/Howlback\|Howlback]], [[Characters/Decepticons/Frenzy\|Frenzy]], and [[Characters/Autobots/Rumble\|Rumble]]. Each of them turns into a small, high-speed aircraft that launches from his alternate mode.

Similarly, most characters who turn into components like heads, engine blocks, etc. are now guns. There are some [[Characters/Neutral/Rewind\|exceptions]] of course.
