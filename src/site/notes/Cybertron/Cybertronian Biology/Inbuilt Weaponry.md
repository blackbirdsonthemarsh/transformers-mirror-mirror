Weaponry that is integrated into one’s frame. It can range from parts of their alt-mode, such as a tank’s turret or a jet’s rotary cannons, to guns that flip out of one’s shoulders, or missile launchers integrated into the chest.

Ammunition for inbuilt weaponry, whether it be in be in the form of super-heated energy or physical projectiles, is generated entirely by the Cybertronian. Laser weapons consume energon from internal reserves, while ammunition for ballistic weapons are built internally from excess metals that are a natural part of the Cybertronian diet. 

Due to how well-integrated most inbuilt weaponry is, the [[Cybertron/Technology/Null-ray\|null-ray]] was invented as a means of disarming ‘bots without resorting to mutilation.
