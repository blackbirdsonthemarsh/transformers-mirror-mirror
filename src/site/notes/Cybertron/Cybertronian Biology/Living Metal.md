A catch-all term for the metals that make up the planet of [[Cybertron\|Cybertron]], and [[Cybertronians\|those born from it]]. Cybertronians are not made from one metal, they are made from many, with the resulting mixtures affecting everything from the color of their optics, to the energon in their veins. Curiously, Cybertronians are capable of converting any metal they eat into living metal, allowing for their frames to naturally repair themselves over time. 

[[Cybertron/Primus and Unicron/Ununtrium\|Ununtrium]] is a kind of living metal, though no Cybertronians have been [[Cybertron/Cybertronian Biology/Forging\|forged]] from it, as it is found miles below the [[Cybertron/Cybertronian Biology/Sparkfield\|sparkfields]] where Cybertronians emerge.
