A Cybertronian medical term used when discussing the different maintenance and fueling needs different kinds of Cybertronian require. A rifle requires different maintenance than a pistol, despite both being guns. A fighter jet and a helicopter are both aircraft, but they work completely differently. Thus, “rifle” and “pistol” are considered separate frametypes, as are “fighter jet” and “helicopter.” 

In the case of Cybertronians who transform into objects or animals, the suffix -former is occasionally appended to their frametype (ex: gunformer, beastformer) to avoid confusion.
