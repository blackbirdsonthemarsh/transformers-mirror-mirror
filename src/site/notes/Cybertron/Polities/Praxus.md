A city-state in [[Cybertron\|Cybertron’s]] northern hemisphere, on the very edge of the [[Cybertron/Locations/Tagan Heights\|Tagan Heights]], right on the shores of the [[Cybertron/Locations/Mithril Sea\|Mithril Sea]]. 

Praxus, like [[Cybertron/Polities/Uraya\|its]] [[Cybertron/Polities/Altihex\|neighbors]] was once a center of manufacturing on Cybertron. In its glory days, it had strong unions and a highly organized working class. Wages were high, working conditions were excellent, and unions would coordinate strikes to keep them that way. This ended with the [[Quintessson War\|Quintessson War]], which saw the bombing of the city, the destruction of much of its infrastructure, and the death of its government. When Praxus joined the [[Cybertron/History/The Primacy/Primacy\|newly-unified Cybertron]], the government that was put in place to replace them had almost nothing in common with its predecessor.

This gave rise to centuries of union-busting, mismanagement, and rampant corruption. This systematic erosion of the power of the working class led to increasingly looser labor laws, and eventually to rapid deindustrialization as the most profitable companies abandoned Praxus for greener pastures, destroying the city’s economy. 

Praxus, ever since the industry that defined it closed up shop, has been seen by the richer, more populated regions as a very humble place, with poor education. Its people are seen as friendly and down-to-earth, if uneducated, a stereotype many of them rankle at— or, [[Characters/Decepticons/Decepticon Intelligence/Prowl\|play up when it suits them.]]

The city survived the [[Cybertron/History/The Great War/Great War\|Great War]] almost untouched, largely due to its unimportance. 
##### Notes
- Praxus is space Ohio. This originally began as a joke, where I decided that the city-state — which often finds itself tragically destroyed — was utterly irrelevant and survived the war unscathed because nobody paid any attention to it. As things related to this continuity tend to do, this spiraled wildly out of control, and now Mirror Mirror Praxus is part of the Cybertronian equivalent of the [Rust Belt](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rust_Belt#). While Praxus is not a one-to-one allegory, nor is its history that of the Rust Belt with the names changed, I have taken some influence from the effects of deindustrialization and the resulting loss of jobs. 
- Characters built or forged in Praxus include: [[Characters/Decepticons/Decepticon Intelligence/Prowl\|Prowl]], [[Barricade\|Barricade]], [[Bluestreak\|Bluestreak]], [[Minerva\|Minerva]], [[Nightbeat\|Nightbeat]], [[Ironhide\|Ironhide]] and [[Crankcase\|Crankcase]].
