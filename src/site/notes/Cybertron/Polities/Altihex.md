A city-state in [[Cybertron\|Cybertron’s]] northern hemisphere. Altihex has had a long and complicated relationship with its nearest neighbor, [[Cybertron/Polities/Uraya\|Uraya]], involving a nigh uncountable amount of wars, invasions and conquests. For as long as they’ve had a national identity, the two city-states have regarded each other as their oldest enemies— though now, that term is used quite affectionately, as fighting on [[Cybertron/History/The Quintesson War/Quintesson War\|the same side against invaders]] followed by millions of years of being part of [[Cybertron/History/The Primacy/Primacy\|the same nation]] caused them to abandon their rivalries long ago. 

During the [[Cybertron/History/The Great War/Great War\|Great War]], the two city-states were officially neutral, but sympathetic to the [[Cybertron/History/The Great War/Factions/Decepticons/Decepticon\|Decepticons]].

Altihex is home to a large research university, which is well-known for its research into improving the energon refining process. While it isn’t as large or well-funded as its counterparts in [[Cybertron/Polities/Vos\|Vos]] and [[Cybertron/Polities/Polyhex\|Polyhex]], it is quite well-respected, and known for its thriving student culture.
