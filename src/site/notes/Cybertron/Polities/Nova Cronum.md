In days past, Nova Cronum was once a military power, though it paled in comparison to the likes of [[Cybertron/Polities/Vos\|Vos]] and [[Cybertron/Polities/Polyhex\|Polyhex]]. Even when it was part of a unified Cybertron, its military legacy remained part of its identity, often to the exclusion of other aspects of its history. Generation after generation was raised to believe in the nobility of their forbearers, and see the military as a pillar of society, for better and for [[Characters/Autobots/Elita One\|worse.]] 

The city was reduced to little more than rubble during the [[Cybertron/History/The Quintesson War/Quintesson War\|Quintesson War]], and though it has long since rebuilt, the scars have yet to fade. 
##### Notes
- Characters built or forged in Nova Cronum include: [[Characters/Autobots/Elita One\|Elita One]], [[Skids\|Skids]], [[Jhiaxus\|Jhiaxus]], [[Megajet\|Megajet]] and [[Characters/Neutral/Rewind\|Rewind]].
- Instead of being hailed a city of thinkers and philosophers, Nova Cronum was once a second-rate military power whose citizens never quite got over that inferiority complex.
