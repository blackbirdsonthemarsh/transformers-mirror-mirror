Tyrest sits at the very edge of the [[Cybertron/Locations/Tagan Heights\|Tagan Heights]], right by the coast of the [[Cybertron/Locations/Mithril Sea\|Mithril Sea]]. It’s an old city, with a vibrant culture and very distinct architecture. It was once considered one of the centers of art and culture, especially the performing arts. Its theater companies and dance troupes were some of the finest on the planet, and the city’s traditional music was famed the world over. However, the city was devastated during the [[Cybertron/History/The Quintesson War/Quintesson War\|Quintesson War]], and never quite managed to recover economically, nor fully rebuild much of its historic architecture. 

Traditionally, it was the artery through which goods from the Tagan Heights made it to the rest of Cybertron. Before the Quintesson War, it was one of the wealthiest city-states on the coast, surpassed only by [[Cybertron/Polities/Kalis\|Kalis]]. However, Tyrest was the seat of cultural power on the coast.  

Despite its fall from prominence, Tyrest remains famous for its art and culture. Its Jekka Amphitheater is a premier destination for theater or dance enthusiasts across the planet, and is one of the oldest amphitheaters on Cybertron that still sees use. It was damaged during the [[Cybertron/History/The Quintesson War/Quintesson War\|Quintesson War]], but rebuilt soon afterwards, and survived the [[Cybertron/History/The Great War/Great War\|Great War]] untouched.
