A [[Cybertron\|Cybertronian]] city-state on the coast of the [[Cybertron/Locations/Mithril Sea\|Mithril Sea]]. Kalis is the largest seaport on Cybertron, and has been the heart of seaborne trade for millions of years. Due to the presence of sea monsters, the Kalisian harbor is heavily defended, enclosed by high walls studded with impressive gun batteries. Battleships and aircraft carriers have always patrolled its waters, to keep transport ships safe from the beasts that lurk below.

Though it has begun to fall out of favor with the generations born after the [[Cybertron/History/The Quintesson War/Quintesson War\|Quintesson War]], monster hunting has long been an essential part of Kalisian culture. It once served as a coming-of-age ritual, with a party’s successful return from a hunt marking their entrance into society. 

Various andministrative around the city are built from the heads of monsters, with the entrance being through the mouth. Buildings in Kalis are painted in bright, eye-catching colors, with detailed patterns around their doorways.

- Characters built or forged in Kalis include: [[Characters/Autobots/Optimus Prime\|Optimus Prime]], [[Characters/Autobots/Ultra Magnus\|Ultra Magnus]], [[Characters/Neutral/Outback\|Outback]], [[Bludgeon\|Bludgeon]] [[Characters/Decepticons/High Command/Thunderblast\|Thunderblast]], [[Esmeral\|Esmeral]], [[Deszaras\|Deszaras]], [[Scylla\|Scylla]] [[Characters/Decepticons/Tidal Wave\|Tidal Wave]], and [[Broadside\|Broadside]]. 
- Kalis has never been associated with the sea or anything aquatic, but I decided that since it borders Tyrest (which is on the edge of a sea, that being the Sea of Rust) that it would be a seaside state in Mirror Mirror. 
- Typically, the most relevant sea in Mirror Mirror is the Sea of Rust. However, outside of Beast Wars: Uprising (and perhaps a few other continuities here and there) it is rarely presented as an actual body of water, instead ranging from an apocalyptic hellscape to an ever-shifting expanse. Due to Mirror Mirror having several prominent boats (hello [[Characters/Decepticons/High Command/Thunderblast\|Thunderblast]]) I decided that its Cybertron would need actual bodies of water. I wanted to keep the Sea of Rust as something more unsettling, so I decided to go with the [Mithril Sea.](https://tfwiki.net/wiki/Mithril_Sea) Outside of its brief appearance in Beast Wars: Uprising, the vast majority of its appearances have been one-off mentions either in dialogue or toy bios, which gave me free reign to do whatever I wanted with it.
