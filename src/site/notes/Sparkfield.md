A field where [[Cybertronians\|Cybertronian]] [[Cybertron/Cybertronian Biology/Spark\|sparks]] emerge from their [[Cybertron\|planet’s]] surface and rest until they are ready to [[Cybertron/Cybertronian Biology/Forging\|become Cybertronians]]. Nearly every major Cybertronian civilization was built around at least one sparkfield, as they are the sole source of Cybertronian life. 

Each sparkfield has unique characteristics, ranging from the metals present (which affect the color of glass, optics and energon, as well as the “base” color the metal returns to upon death) to the rate at which sparks emerge and ignite. Some sparkfields produce [[Cybertron/Cybertronian Biology/Split-spark Siblings\|split sparks]] and [[Cybertron/Cybertronian Biology/Outlier\|outliers]] at a higher rate than others. 

The natural emergence rate of even the highest-producing sparkfield is incredibly low, though when combined with the lifespans of Cybertronians, the low “birth” rate keeps the population stable.
